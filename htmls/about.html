About Joshua

Hey, I'm Joshua.

I'm Joshua Mendoza — a GoHighLevel Expert, AI Automation Specialist, and Web Developer based in Antipolo City, Philippines. I co-founded NexSale Digital Marketing alongside my brother Jonathan and two close friends from our missions.

My technical foundation comes from studying full-stack web development, covering both front-end and back-end engineering. I've also completed GoHighLevel certification and Meta Ads certification — keeping my skills sharp and verified.

Over 3+ years, I've built systems for HVAC companies, business coaches, roofing contractors, e-commerce stores, and digital marketing agencies. In 2026 I joined Rayanova Agency and Regas Media, handling client onboarding, loading GHL snapshots, connecting AI chatbots via Appointwise, and setting up Zapier and Slack integrations. I also run Google and Meta Ads campaigns, creating video and image ad creatives using AI-powered tools and API integrations.

Most recently, I built a fully autonomous Social Media AI Agent using Claude Code in VS Code — a system that researches trending topics via Genspark and Gemini, generates visuals with Fal.ai and Nanobanana, then publishes AI-crafted posts across every major platform in a single click using Blotato and Postiz.
