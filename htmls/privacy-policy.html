Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information this website ("the Site," operated by Joshua Mendoza / NexSale Digital Marketing) collects, how it's used, and the choices you have. By using this Site, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

The Site collects information only when you choose to provide it — specifically, through the contact form on the Contact page. That form asks for:

  • Your first and last name
  • Your email address
  • The service you're interested in (optional dropdown)
  • Your project details / message

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data through this Site. The Site does not use cookies, analytics scripts, or advertising trackers of any kind.

2. How Your Information Is Used

Contact form submissions are used solely to respond to your inquiry — to understand your project, follow up by email or WhatsApp, and provide a quote or proposal if relevant. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third-party marketers.

3. Third-Party Services

The contact form is processed using EmailJS, a third-party service that securely delivers form submissions to our inbox without routing them through a custom backend server. EmailJS may process your name, email, and message as part of that delivery. You can review EmailJS's own privacy practices at their website.

Some portfolio case studies on this Site link out to client websites or GoHighLevel-hosted preview pages. Those are independently operated, and this Privacy Policy does not cover their data practices — please review their own policies before submitting information on those sites.

4. Data Retention

Messages submitted through the contact form are retained in our email inbox and EmailJS dashboard for as long as needed to respond to your inquiry and maintain a record of past client communication, unless you request earlier deletion.

5. Your Rights

You may request, at any time, to:

  • See what information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Have your information deleted from our records

To make any of these requests, email shuakai15@gmail.com and we'll respond within a reasonable timeframe.

6. Children's Privacy

This Site is intended for business inquiries and is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in how the Site operates. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision.
